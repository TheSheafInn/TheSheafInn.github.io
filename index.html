Welcome to Our Site The Sheaf and its heritage!

Reopened in September 2018, The Sheaf Inn is a crucial element to the village of West Haddon. Offering food and drinks to patrons within a mixture of open and cosy spaces, The Sheaf Inn will surely accomodate anyone!

The Sheaf Inn has a total of three different inside sceneries to offer, having a snug area at the front, moving to a more open bar area, and eventually reaching a wonderful airy seating area for dining. The Sheaf Inn also offers an outside area with plenty of room to enjoy the sunny weather in the summer.
